Welcome to Happy Trader 🎉

Happy Trader is your command center for evaluating and optimizing your options trading strategies.

Use the interactive dashboards to analyze your positions, review strategy performance, and uncover what is or isn’t working. Whether you're fine-tuning your delta exposure, tracking position outcomes, or preparing for earnings season, Happy Trader brings your trading data to life.

Features

  • 📊 Strategy analysis by account and symbol
  • 📈 Position-level insights: gain/loss, earnings dates, ex-dividends
  • 🧠 Understand which strategies are actually working — and which aren’t
  • 🔍 Filter by symbol, account, or strategy to drill into the data

Happy Trader helps you become a smarter, more confident trader.
