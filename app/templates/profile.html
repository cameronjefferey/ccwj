Broker sync (via SnapTrade)

Schwab, Fidelity, Vanguard, Robinhood, IBKR, Alpaca, Wealthsimple, and others through the SnapTrade Connection Portal. Connect once per broker; we handle history and daily refresh from then on.

What to expect after you connect:
  • Within minutes: your current positions, cash balance, and account total.
  • Within a few hours: recent trade history (sometimes up to 24h for the very first sync of a new connection).
SnapTrade pulls trade history from each broker on its own schedule — we can't speed that up. If trades aren't visible right away, sync again later. They will appear; we've never seen a connected broker fail to backfill.
