About the data lag — please read once:
SnapTrade pulls positions and balances in real time (within seconds of a sync). Trade history is a separate ingestion that SnapTrade runs against each broker on its own schedule — typically a few hours, sometimes up to 24 hours for a brand-new connection or for a trade you just placed. If you see "0 trades" right after connecting or placing a trade, that's SnapTrade still indexing, not data loss. Sync again later — the trades will appear.
