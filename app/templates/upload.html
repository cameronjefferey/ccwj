Upload Portfolio Data

Export your trade history and current positions from Charles Schwab (schwab.com), then upload the CSV files here. Data will refresh in a few minutes.

Supported broker: Charles Schwab (web only — not thinkorswim)
How to export from Schwab

Trade history

  1. Log in at schwab.com → Accounts → History → Transactions
  2. Select your account from the dropdown
  3. Set filter to All Transactions
  4. Choose a date range and click Search
  5. Click Export → CSV → Save the file

Current positions

  1. Log in at schwab.com → Accounts → Positions
  2. Select your account from the dropdown
  3. Click Export → OK → Save the file
